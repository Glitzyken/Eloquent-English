Objective

The Objective of this book is to help anybody reading it to:

  • acquire good communication skills in English Language
  • know how to speak English Language with confidence and clarity, thus becoming the best you can be through your speaking power
  • know and abide by the rules guiding English word pronunciations
  • speak with correct diction always

The only thing required of you to use this book is your desire to learn.
