chapter 1
communication

what is communication?

Communication is simply the act of sending and receiving messages through verbal or non-verbal means.

Now that we have known what communication is, we need to know what effective communication entails. If communication is effective, there will be mutual understanding.

Effective communication is a reciprocal process which involves the sending party sending a message that iseasily understood by the receiving party. Effective communication entails not just the way you use words, italso covers different skills such as non-verbal communication, ability to understand your own emotions aswell as those of the other person you are communicating with.

how to attain the skills of effective communication

To be an effective communicator, you need to attain the skills of effective communication.

active listening

This can be done by listening as much as you speak. You need to listen with your whole body, be alert and interested in the other person. Also, refrain from interrupting and reflecting on what you have heard.

Ralph Nichols states that “the most basic of all human needs is the need to understand and be understood”. The best way to understand people is listening to them.

ask questions

To improve your communication skills, you need to ask questions when you are conversing with someone. When you ask questions, the communication keeps flowing.

being clear and succinct

When you are speaking, be articulate and concise. Clarity is important when speaking English.

none-verbal communication

When communicating, take note of your nonverbal communication signals such as tone of voice, eye contact, facial expressions and the posture of your arms and legs. We have seen how we can attain the skill of an effective communicator. It is proper we know why effective communication is important.

Importance of Effective Communication

  • Effective communication is important for the development of an organization and personal lives.
  • Effective communication helps in adjusting people’s attitude. A well-informed person will always have a better manner of speaking than a less informed person.
  • Effective communication serves as a means of socialization.
  • It deepens connection with people.
  • It improves teamwork
  • It helps to avoid conflict.
